Rajiv is a software engineer at A.P. Moller - Maersk who is passionate about using technology to solve real-world problems. He primarily works on backend systems and infrastructure, with strong interests in algorithms and distributed systems. An active open-source enthusiast, Rajiv participated in Google Season of Docs (2020) with gRPC-Gateway, Google Season of Docs (2021) with Wechaty, LFX Mentorship (Fall 2021) with moja global, and Google Summer of Code (2022) with Keptn, and is also involved with Jenkins through Google Summer of Code, where he has been volunteering as a Google Summer of Code mentor since 2023, supporting and guiding new contributors in the open-source community.
